Bel Rolón | Pegasoul
Hi, there. I'm Bel, art teacher and illustrator from San Nicolás de los Arroyos, Argentina, currently working towards becoming a graphic designer and front-end developer.
Art is a subject that has always held a significant place in my life. Since my childhood, drawing has been my favorite and most cherished hobby. I'm happy I've kept it to the present day, and I hope it remains with me until the very end.
In the meantime, I've created this website for all of you who are curious about my work. Thank you so much for stopping by ☆
